Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

Octile ("the App") is a puzzle game developed by an independent developer. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

The App collects minimal data solely to support the World Scoreboard feature:

  • Anonymous Player ID — A randomly generated UUID stored on your device. This is not linked to your name, email, or any personal account.
  • Puzzle Completion Data — When you solve a puzzle, the puzzle number and your solve time are sent to our server to display on the scoreboard.
  • Basic Device Info — Operating system and browser/app version, used for debugging and compatibility.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • No location data
  • No financial or payment information
  • No contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • No advertising identifiers

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in the App.

Data Storage and Security

Scoreboard data is stored on a server operated by the developer. The anonymous player ID is stored locally on your device. All network communication uses HTTPS encryption.

Data Retention

Scoreboard data is retained indefinitely to maintain historical rankings. You can reset your local player ID at any time by clearing the app's data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App only collects anonymous, non-personal data, it is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please email us at octile.app@gmail.com or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
