Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

Octile ("the App") is a puzzle game developed by an independent developer. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Without an account (anonymous play):

  • Anonymous Player ID — A randomly generated UUID stored on your device. This is not linked to your name, email, or any personal account.
  • Puzzle Completion Data — When you solve a puzzle, the puzzle number, your solve time, and the solved board state are sent to our server for the scoreboard and to verify the solution is valid.
  • Device Info — Basic technical request information (such as browser type) may be recorded server-side for debugging and compatibility. This information is not used to identify users.

With an account (optional sign-in):

  • Email Address — If you create an account using email, your email is stored for authentication purposes (login, password reset). We send a one-time verification code to confirm your email.
  • Display Name — A name you choose, shown on the scoreboard and your profile.
  • Google Account Info — If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google email, display name, and profile picture via OAuth. We do not access your contacts, calendar, or any other Google data.
  • Gameplay Progress — When signed in, your level progress, achievements, EXP, diamonds, ELO rating, grades, and streak data are synced to our server so you can access them across devices.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No location data
  • No financial or payment information (diamonds are earned in-game, not purchased with real money)
  • No contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data from users who do not create an account, beyond the anonymous scoreboard data described above

Third-Party Services

The App uses Cloudflare Turnstile, an invisible bot detection service, to protect the scoreboard from automated abuse. Turnstile verifies that requests come from a real browser — it does not collect personal data, display CAPTCHAs, or track users across sites. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

The App uses Google OAuth as an optional sign-in method. When you choose to sign in with Google, the authentication flow is handled by Google. We only receive your email, display name, and profile picture. See Google's Privacy Policy.

The App does not use any advertising, analytics, or other tracking services. There are no ads in the App.

Data Storage and Security

Scoreboard data and account data are stored on a server operated by the developer. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Authentication uses JWT tokens. All network communication uses HTTPS encryption.

Game progress is stored locally on your device. When signed in, progress is also synced to our server using a MAX-merge strategy (highest values are always preserved).

Account Deletion

You can sign out at any time from the Profile screen, which clears all game data from your local device. To request deletion of your server-side account data, please email us at octileapp@googlegroups.com.

Data Retention

Scoreboard and account data are retained indefinitely to maintain historical rankings and cross-device sync. You can reset your local player ID at any time by clearing the app's data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Account creation requires an email address. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please email us at octileapp@googlegroups.com or use our feedback form.

隱私權政策

生效日期：2026 年 3 月 31 日

Octile（以下簡稱「本應用」）是一款由獨立開發者開發的拼圖遊戲。本政策說明本應用收集哪些資料及其用途。

我們收集的資料

未建立帳號（匿名遊玩）：

  • 匿名玩家 ID — 儲存在您裝置上的隨機生成 UUID。此 ID 不與您的姓名、電子郵件或任何個人帳號關聯。
  • 解謎資料 — 當您完成謎題時，謎題編號、解題時間和棋盤狀態會傳送至我們的伺服器，用於排行榜和驗證解答是否正確。
  • 裝置資訊 — 基本的技術請求資訊（如瀏覽器類型）可能會在伺服器端記錄，用於除錯和相容性。此資訊不用於識別使用者。

建立帳號後（可選登入）：

  • 電子郵件地址 — 如果您使用電子郵件建立帳號，您的電子郵件將用於驗證（登入、密碼重設）。我們會傳送一次性驗證碼以確認您的電子郵件。
  • 顯示名稱 — 您選擇的名稱，顯示在排行榜和個人檔案上。
  • Google 帳號資訊 — 如果您使用 Google 登入，我們會透過 OAuth 取得您的 Google 電子郵件、顯示名稱和個人頭像。我們不會存取您的聯絡人、行事曆或任何其他 Google 資料。
  • 遊戲進度 — 登入後，您的關卡進度、成就、經驗值、鑽石、ELO 排名、評分和連續登入資料會同步至我們的伺服器，以便您在不同裝置間存取。

我們不收集的資料

  • 不收集位置資料
  • 不收集財務或支付資訊（鑽石透過遊戲內獲得，非真實貨幣購買）
  • 不收集您裝置上的聯絡人、照片或檔案
  • 不收集廣告識別碼
  • 不收集未建立帳號使用者的資料（除上述匿名排行榜資料外）

第三方服務

本應用使用 Cloudflare Turnstile（一種隱形機器人偵測服務）來保護排行榜免受自動化濫用。Turnstile 驗證請求是否來自真實瀏覽器 — 它不收集個人資料、不顯示驗證碼、不跨網站追蹤使用者。請參閱 Cloudflare 隱私權政策。

本應用使用 Google OAuth 作為可選的登入方式。當您選擇使用 Google 登入時，驗證流程由 Google 處理。我們僅接收您的電子郵件、顯示名稱和個人頭像。請參閱 Google 隱私權政策。

本應用不使用任何廣告、分析或其他追蹤服務。應用內沒有廣告。

資料儲存與安全

排行榜資料和帳號資料儲存在開發者運營的伺服器上。密碼經過雜湊處理，從不以明文儲存。身份驗證使用 JWT 令牌。所有網路通訊均使用 HTTPS 加密。

遊戲進度儲存在您的本地裝置上。登入後，進度也會使用 MAX 合併策略（始終保留最高值）同步至我們的伺服器。

帳號刪除

您可以隨時從個人檔案頁面登出，這會清除本地裝置上的所有遊戲資料。如需刪除伺服器端的帳號資料，請發送電子郵件至 octileapp@googlegroups.com。

資料保留

排行榜和帳號資料將無限期保留，以維護歷史排名和跨裝置同步。您可以隨時透過清除應用資料來重設本地玩家 ID。

兒童隱私

本應用不會故意收集 13 歲以下兒童的個人資訊。建立帳號需要電子郵件地址。如果您認為有兒童提供了個人資訊，請聯繫我們。

政策變更

我們可能會不時更新本隱私權政策。變更將在此頁面發布並更新生效日期。

聯繫方式

如您對本隱私權政策有疑問，請發送電子郵件至 octileapp@googlegroups.com，或使用我們的意見回饋表單。
