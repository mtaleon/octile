# Octile — Steam Store Page

## Short Description (< 300 chars)

A meditative tiling puzzle where you fill an 8x8 board with 11 interlocking pieces. 1,000+ handcrafted puzzles across 4 difficulty levels. 15 art themes from Stained Glass to Cyberpunk. Keyboard-first controls. Zen Mode for distraction-free solving.

## About This Game

### The Puzzle

Every board is an 8x8 grid with 3 pieces already placed. Your job: fit the remaining 8 colored tiles so every cell is filled, nothing overlaps, and nothing is left empty. Each piece can be rotated. Every puzzle has exactly one solution.

It sounds simple. It isn't.

### Four Difficulty Levels

- **Easy** — Gentle introduction. Par time: 60 seconds.
- **Medium** — Tighter fits, more rotations needed. Par: 90 seconds.
- **Hard** — Multiple false paths. Par: 120 seconds.
- **Nightmare** — For the obsessive. Par: 180 seconds.

1,000+ puzzles and growing. Play in order or jump to a random challenge.

### 15 Visual Themes

Transform the board into a work of art. 3 free themes (Classic, LEGO, Wood) and 12 premium themes unlockable through gameplay — no real-money purchases:

Stained Glass | Marble & Gold | Quilt | Deep Sea | Space Galaxy | Botanical | Cyberpunk | Ancient Ink | Ukiyo-e | Steampunk | Frozen | Halloween

Each theme changes the entire visual experience — board, pieces, borders, and ambient feel.

### Zen Mode

Press `Z` to strip away all interface chrome. No timer. No stats. Just the board, the pieces, and your thoughts. Header and controls fade back in when you hover. This is Octile at its most focused — the puzzle breathing on its own.

### Full Keyboard Support

Every action has a keyboard shortcut. Select pieces with `1-8`, rotate with `R`, navigate the board with arrow keys, place with `Enter`. Undo with `Backspace`. No mouse required — solve entire puzzles from the keyboard.

### Grades & Mastery

Every solve earns a grade: **S** (under par, no hints), **A** (fast or clean), or **B** (completed). Chase S-ranks for double EXP. Track your progress across difficulty levels, build streaks, and unlock 65 achievements.

### Daily Challenges

3 random tasks each day — solve puzzles, earn grades, try harder levels. Complete all three for bonus rewards. Compete in the Team League across 8 gemstone tiers from Bronze to Obsidian.

### Works Offline

Full offline support. Puzzles, themes, and progress all saved locally. Scores sync when you reconnect. Pick up exactly where you left off.
